Research Focus

My research integrates psychology, gerontology and nursing, focusing on cognitive health, caregiving wellbeing, successful aging, mindfulness-based, nature-based and technology-based interventions.

Cognitive Aging

Investigating cognitive functions, prospective memory, and successful aging trajectories in older adults.

Interventions

Developing mindfulness-based, nature-based and technology-based interventions to improve mental well-being.

Methodology

Specializing in randomized-controlled trials (RCT), mixed methods research, and panel data analyses.

Big Data Analysis

Utilizing big data including ELSA and CHARLS to understand aging trajectories to promote successful aging.
